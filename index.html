Legend of Zelda: Tears of the Kingdom


In Tears of the Kingdom, Link and Zelda explored below Hyrule castle to possibly uncover an explanation for the weird occurences in their Kingdom. In the process of exploring, they uncover a Mummified looking corpse being guarded by a glowing hand. Their presence accidentally faded the power of the hand and awakened the corpse of who turns out to be the demon king. The demon king then attacks the both of them decaying the Master Sword, weakening Link, decaying his right arm, and causes the chamber to collapse which leads to the dissappearance of Princess Zelda. Now without his sword, stamina, and heart levels, Link must save Hyrule and Find Princess Zelda

Characters Information
Link
  • Species: Hylian
  • Gender: Male
  • Description: Link, otherwise known as the hero of Hyrule, is often depicted as a boy or young man that is the appointed Knight of Princess Zelda. He was blessed by the Golden Goddesses and possesses the spirit of the hero meaning his destiny is to fight evil and protect all the Citizens of Hyrule.

    • In Tears of the Kingdom, due to his right armed being weakend, he was blessed by the ghost of Rauru, the first king of Hyrule, and gained Rauru's arm along with perks to assist him in saving Hyrule.
Zelda
  • Species: Hylian
  • Gender: Female
  • Description: Zelda, The Princess of Hyrule and Reincarnation of the goddess Hylia. In most timelines, she has magical powers such as, healing, precognition, and teleportation which can be seen throughout Tears of the Kingdom.

    • To return the Master Sword to its full potential and recover it faster, Zelda ate the secret stone that was entrusted to her which turned into a dragon known as the "Dragon of Light" and embeded the Master Sword in her forehead for it to absorb all her energy in order for her to restore it.
Purah
  • Species: Sheikah
  • Gender: Female
  • Description: Purah, an inventor, researcher, and a member of the Sheikah tribe-- a race of humanoids who hold ancient powers and are responsible for creating advanced technology--. She is also the older sister of Impa, Princess Zelda's former advisor and bodyguard. Other than this, despite Purah being 124 years old, she still manages to maintain the shape of a young woman; in Breath of the Wild she could be seen in an even younger form where she takes the shape of a child.

    • She is the Head of Lookout Landing--a research station trying to collect data on the gloom and exploring the depths to further understand it-- She is responsible for the creation of skyview towers, a tower that allows you to scan the area of the surface as well as the sky islands of a certain region by launching you in the air.
Master Kohga
  • Species: Sheikah
  • Gender: Male
  • Description: Master Kohga is the leader of the Yiga Clan; a group of rebel Sheikah assasins and theives who are known to steal items and information from the Gerudo Tribe. The symbol of the Yiga is the sheikah symbol flipped upside down and their hair is mostly darker colors to show rebellion against the Sheikah Tribe and their ideals. And due to Link's Lengthy History of Terrorizing them and their hideouts in order to protect Hyrule, the Yiga Clan hate him and do whatever they can to kill him, such as disguising as travelers and attacking him upon interaction.

    • Being the Leader of the Yiga Clan, he has the strongest powers among all the members and can be found deep within Yiga Hideouts. When found by Link, he creates obstacles to protect himself as well as attempt his goal of killing the Hero of Hyrule once and for all.
Ganondorf
  • Species: Gerudo
  • Gender: Male
  • Description: Ganondorf, The only known male Gerudo, is the reincarnation of Demise, a former god that represents chaos. Ganondorf unleashes evil using the power of the blood moon which resurrects all the beasts and take control of Hyrule.

    • This particular version of Ganondorf is the reincarnated human form of Calamity Ganon (an entity from the previous game) In Tears of The Kingdom, he eats the secret stone of darkness, boosting his power and turning him into Demon King Ganondorf.
